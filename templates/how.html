How to Use Editofy

Follow these simple steps to edit your images online with Editofy:

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Click on the "Choose File" button to select the image you want to edit from your device. Make sure your image is in one of the supported formats (webp, png, jpg, jpeg, gif).

Step 2: Select the Editing Operation

Choose the desired editing operation from the dropdown menu. You can convert your image to different formats (PNG, JPG, WEBP), convert it to grayscale, blur it, sharpen it, or apply edge detection.

Step 3: Submit for Processing

Once you have selected the operation, click the "Submit" button. Your image will be processed and the edited version will be made available.

Step 4: Download the Processed Image

After processing, a link to download your edited image will be provided. Click on the download link to save the processed image to your device.
